üìã House Rules / Normandy House Manual

This space is designed for people who value tranquility, respect, and community. In the event of any inconvenience, we ask guests to remain calm and address the issue respectfully. We recognize that unforeseen setbacks may arise, but we ask for a collaborative and constructive attitude to find solutions. We appreciate the understanding and patience of everyone involved in such situations.

üîπ General Rules

1. Respect and coexistence

This is a shared space. Respect for other guests, neighbors, and hosts is expected.

  • Any behavior that affects the harmony or tranquility of the place is not permitted.

2. Quiet hours

  • From 10:00 p.m. the hours of silence apply.
  • Avoid loud music, laughter, or any noise that might bother others.

üî• Important Prohibitions

1. Smoking ban

  • Smoking is prohibited inside the accommodation (including cigarettes, electronic cigarettes, rolled tobacco, or any variant).
  • If you wish to smoke, please do so only in designated outdoor areas.
  • Failure to comply with this rule will result in additional charges for deep cleaning and/or replacement of smoke-damaged furniture.

2. Candles, incense and open fire

  • Lighting candles, incense, or any object with fire or embers is not permitted within the space.
  • This measure prevents fires, wax stains and lingering odors.
  • Damages caused by non-compliance will be charged to the responsible guest.

ü™ë Space Care

1. Furniture and objects

  • Many of the pieces of furniture have not only economic, but also sentimental value.
  • We ask you to use them with care and responsibility.
  • Any damage must be reported immediately.
  • Unreported damage will incur a charge for the full replacement value.

2. Cleaning Service and Cleanliness

  • During the stay, cleaning and changing of sheets are carried out only at the end of the reservation.
  • When the accommodation is vacant, our team performs maintenance cleaning every three days, ensuring you always receive a spotless space.
  • For long stays of more than 20 days, we offer a Free mid-stay cleaning service (starting on the 15th), which is coordinated in advance for greater convenience.
  • Avoid using substances that generate strong odors (e.g., burnt oils, incense, smoke).
  • If additional deep cleaning is necessary due to odors, the responsible guest will be charged.

Courtesy Articles

At the beginning of your stay you will find toilet paper, soap (hand and body) and shampoo. These products are initial courtesy. If supplies run out, the guest will be responsible for purchasing what is necessary to meet their needs for the remainder of the stay.

üìã Other Indications

  1. Guest Registration: All guests must be registered, just like the main guests. Please send a copy of their ID.
  2. Quiet Hours Policy: A quiet hour policy is in place between 9:30 p.m. and 8:00 a.m. We ask that guests respect the rest of the neighbors and keep noise to a minimum during these hours, including lowering the television volume and avoiding noisy shoes.
  3. Pets are prohibited without prior consent: Pets are not allowed in the accommodation without prior authorization.
  4. Not Suitable for Children or Infants: The accommodation is not suitable for children or infants. Minors are not allowed.
  5. Security: Make sure you properly lock and close windows when leaving the accommodation.
  6. Television and Internet: If the apartment you rented has a television, each guest must use their own login information for platforms like Netflix and others. The internet connection is active and should be working properly.
  7. Cleaning Service: We perform a minimum cleaning every three days and whenever a guest checks out, including changing sheets and blankets.

‚úÖ Consequences

Failure to comply with these rules may result in:

  • Economic charge for cleaning, repair or replacement.
  • Early cancellation of the reservation without refund, in serious cases.

These rules are designed to ensure a comfortable and pleasant stay for all guests, as well as to maintain respect for the property and neighbors. Thank you for your cooperation!

üêæ Pet Policy at Casa Normand√≠a House

At Normand√≠a House, we love welcoming the whole family, including your four-legged friends! To ensure a pleasant and safe stay for everyone, we've established the following pet policies:

1. One Pet Per Unit

Only one dog or cat is allowed per reservation to maintain the property's tranquility. Other pets must be discussed with us in advance.

2. Leash Requirement

Dogs must always be on a leash within the property.

3. Supervision

Dogs and cats cannot be left alone in the studio or common areas.

4. No Furniture

To maintain hygiene, your dog or cat is not allowed on beds, sofas, or other furniture.

5. Clean-Up

Guests must clean up after their pets using their own supplies, such as waste bags.

6. Outdoor Walks

Although we have a front garden, your pet cannot use this space for their needs. They must be taken off the property for walks.

7. Behavior

Only well-behaved, non-aggressive, and non-destructive dogs and cats are allowed. Excessive barking, constant meowing, or other disruptive behaviors are not permitted.

8. Health

Dogs and cats must be clean, free of fleas or ticks, and up-to-date on all vaccinations.

9. Additional Charges

Extra charges may apply for additional cleaning or damages caused by your pet.

10. Non-Compliance

Failure to adhere to these rules may result in additional charges or cancellation of your reservation.

11. Exotic Animal Prohibition

For safety and well-being, exotic animals such as reptiles, snakes, spiders, or other unconventional pets are not allowed.

12. Authorization for Non-Conventional Pets

If you wish to bring a pet other than a dog or cat, you must discuss this with us in advance. This includes animals like rodents, birds, or other small species.

13. Safe Transport

In the case of approved non-conventional pets, they must be transported in secure cages and supervised at all times.

14. Extra Deposit or Charge

For special cases, an extra deposit may be required to cover potential damages or additional inspections after the stay.

15. Reservation Rejection

We reserve the right to reject reservations if we believe the pet is not suitable for the property or could compromise other guests' experiences.

By bringing your pet, you agree to follow these rules and take full responsibility for their behavior during the stay. If you need anything additional for your pet, such as recommendations for pet-friendly places, don't hesitate to let us know. üêæ‚ú®
