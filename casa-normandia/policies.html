Casa Normandía Policies

This space is designed for professionals and digital nomads who value tranquility, respect, and productivity. Learn our rules and policies to ensure a productive and pleasant stay for everyone.

? House Rules ? Pet Policy ? Guest Registration

? House Rules

This space is designed for professionals and digital nomads who value tranquility, respect, and productivity. In case of any inconvenience, we ask guests to remain calm and address the issue respectfully and professionally.

? General Rules

1. Respect and Professionalism

This is a shared space. Respect for other guests, neighbors, and hosts is expected.

  • Avoid behavior that affects the harmony or tranquility of the place
  • Maintain a professional and productive environment

2. Quiet Hours

  • From 10:00 p.m. strict silence hours apply
  • Avoid music, loud calls, or any noise that might disturb others
  • We prioritize deep rest for maximum productivity

? Important Prohibitions

Smoking Ban
  • Smoking is prohibited inside the accommodation (including cigarettes, e-cigarettes, rolled tobacco, or any variant)
  • If you wish to smoke, please do so only in designated outdoor areas
  • Failure to comply will result in additional charges for deep cleaning and/or replacement of smoke-damaged furniture
Candles, Incense and Open Fire
  • Lighting candles, incense, or any object with fire or embers is not permitted within the space
  • This measure prevents fires, wax stains and lingering odors
  • Damages caused by non-compliance will be charged to the responsible guest

? Consequences

Failure to comply with these rules may result in:

  • Economic charges for cleaning, repair, or replacement
  • Early cancellation without refund in serious cases

? Pet Policy

At Casa Normandía we can welcome your four-legged friend under specific conditions. To ensure a pleasant and safe stay for everyone, we have established the following policies:

1. One Pet per Unit

Only one dog or cat per reservation is allowed to maintain tranquility.

2. Leash Required

Dogs must always be on leash within the property.

3. Constant Supervision

Pets cannot be left alone in the studio or common areas.

4. No Furniture

Your pet cannot get on beds, sofas, or other furniture.

5. Immediate Cleanup

Guests must pick up their pet's waste.

6. External Walks

Needs must be done outside the property.

Exotic Animals Prohibition

For safety and welfare reasons, exotic animals such as reptiles, snakes, spiders, or other unconventional pets are not allowed.

By bringing your pet, you accept to follow these rules and assume full responsibility for their behavior during the stay.
